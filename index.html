Vision Beyond the Lens

The ‘why’ of my photography

Lately, the simple act of picking up my camera has felt like an insurmountable task. Life’s relentless demands coupled with personal challenges have cast a shadow over my creative pursuits. Yet, amidst the chaos, I find solace in revisiting the fundamental question: why do I photograph?

Embracing Authenticity

In a world that often measures success by external standards, the journey to discovering your true calling may involve unbecoming what isn’t you. Embracing authenticity, questioning societal norms, and cultivating resilience are all crucial steps on the path to self-discovery. Remember, it’s never too late to redefine your aspirations and align your journey with the person you are meant to become.
